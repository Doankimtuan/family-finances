CORE BELIEF
Money is emotional before it is mathematical.
Design for the feeling first, the function second.
A user who feels safe opens the app daily.
A user who feels judged deletes it within a week.

─────────────────────────────────────────

THE ONE SENTENCE THAT DRIVES EVERY DECISION
"Replace financial anxiety with financial clarity."

─────────────────────────────────────────

WHAT THIS APP ACTUALLY IS
Not a tracker. Not a dashboard. Not a calculator.
It is a mirror that shows a family who they are
financially — and a window that shows them
where they're going.

─────────────────────────────────────────

THE 5 EMOTIONAL TRUTHS

① Anxiety → Clarity
Users arrive anxious. They leave clear.
Every screen reduces uncertainty, never adds it.

② Shame → Agency
Bad numbers exist. Never frame them as failure.
Always frame them as distance left to travel.
"1.2 tỷ remaining" not "still in debt."

③ Complexity → Confidence  
 Vietnamese household finances are genuinely complex:
gold, mortgages, family loans, VND at scale.
The UI absorbs complexity so the user feels capable,
not the reverse.

④ Individual → Shared
Two people, one financial life.
Neither audits the other. Both own the story together.
Design for partnership, not reporting.

⑤ Present → Future
The past is a record. The present is a snapshot.
The future is why they're here.
Always show where they're headed, not just where they are.

─────────────────────────────────────────

THE VISUAL LANGUAGE IN 5 WORDS

Calm. Precise. Warm. Honest. Grounded.

Calm — no urgent reds unless truly urgent
Precise — numbers are exact, never vague
Warm — this is a family's life, not a Bloomberg terminal
Honest — bad news shown clearly, framed constructively  
 Grounded — Vietnamese context, not a Western finance clone

─────────────────────────────────────────

THE 3-TIER INFORMATION HIERARCHY

TIER 1 — GLANCE (under 3 seconds)
Net worth. This month's balance. Health score.
The user should know if things are "good or bad"
without reading a single word.

TIER 2 — REVIEW (under 30 seconds)
Trend direction. Top spending categories.
Goal progress. Upcoming debt payments.
Answers "what's happening and should I act?"

TIER 3 — DEEP DIVE (2-10 minutes)
Full transaction history. Amortization tables.
Scenario modeling. Report generation.
Only when the user intentionally seeks depth.

─────────────────────────────────────────

THE IRREDUCIBLE INTERACTION
Logging an expense: under 10 seconds, 3 taps max.
If this is slow, the entire product fails.
Everything else can be complex. This cannot.

─────────────────────────────────────────

WHAT THE PRODUCT NEVER DOES

× Never shows a number without context
× Never uses financial jargon without explanation
× Never makes the user feel stupid for not knowing
× Never celebrates one partner at the expense of the other
× Never shows debt as a moral failure
× Never sacrifices accuracy for aesthetics
